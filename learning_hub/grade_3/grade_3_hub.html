The Value Secret (Place Value)

Every number up to **1000** has three spots: **Hundreds, Tens, and Ones**. The spot (or place) of a digit tells you its true value.

  • In **425**: The '4' is worth **400** (4 Hundreds).
  • The '2' is worth **20** (2 Tens).
  • The '5' is worth **5** (5 Ones).

Building & Breaking Numbers (Composing/Decomposing)

You can build numbers by putting the expanded form together, or break them down to see their parts:

**Example:** $700 + 80 + 3 = 783$

Who is Bigger? (Comparing & Ordering)

To tell if a number is bigger (>) or smaller (<), always check the **Hundreds place first**.

**Rule:** We use the symbols: **>** (Greater Than), **<** (Less Than), and **=** (Equal To).

**Example:** $915$ is bigger than $450$ because $9$ hundreds is more than $4$ hundreds.

Estimation & Rounding

Rounding helps you guess a close answer before you solve!

**Tip:** If the digit next door is **5 or higher (5, 6, 7, 8, 9)**, you **round up** the target number.

**Example:** $237$ rounded to the nearest ten is $240$.

Multiplication & Division Power

You are becoming a master of **Math Facts**!

**Facts to Know:** Practice multiplying by **2, 5, and 10**!

  • $5 \times 7 = 35$
  • The **Inverse Power**: If $5 \times 7 = 35$, then $35 \div 5 = 7$!

The Fraction Crew (Fair Share)

Fractions happen when you share things equally. If 2 friends share 1 pizza, they each get $\frac{1}{2}$.

**Equivalency Magic:** Some fractions look different but mean the same amount!

The Pattern Rule

A pattern is a rule that repeats. To solve a pattern, you must find the rule!

**Rule Types:**

  • **Repeating:** (A, B, A, B, A...) $\rightarrow$ 1, 5, 1, 5, ?
  • **Growing:** The numbers get bigger. $\rightarrow$ 3, 6, 9, 12, ? (Rule: Add 3)
  • **Shrinking:** The numbers get smaller. $\rightarrow$ 50, 40, 30, ? (Rule: Subtract 10)
